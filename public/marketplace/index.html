Workflow

Browse. Fork. Compose. Ship.

Every marketplace artifact is a standard Weblisk blueprint. No special formats, no lock-in, no hidden dependencies.

1. Browse

Search by category, language target, compliance tier, or keyword. Every listing shows specification counts, dependency maps, and validation status.

2. Fork

Fork any blueprint into your project. Customize it to match your domain, security requirements, and deployment targets. Your fork, your rules.

3. Compose

Compose marketplace blueprints with your own. Dependency contracts are explicit — the framework validates compatibility at every stage.

4. Generate & Ship

Generate production code from the composed blueprints. Same deterministic output, same governance enforcement, same validation pipeline.
